🧠 Math Tricks

  • Multiplication by 11: 23 × 11 = 253 (2 + 3 in middle)
  • Square ending with 5: 35² = 3×4 = 12 → 1225
  • Multiply by 5: 86 × 5 = 86 ÷ 2 = 43 → 430
  • Square near 100: 98² = 9604 (100-2 rule)
  • Division by 5: 145 ÷ 5 = 145 × 2 ÷ 10 = 29
  • Quick addition: 498 + 376 = (500 + 376) - 2 = 874
  • Multiply by 9: 7×9 = 63 (10×7 - 7)
  • Percentage shortcut: 50% = 1/2, 25% = 1/4, 20% = 1/5
  • Fast percentage form: 20% increase = ×1.2

🧠 Special Trick: Triangular Matrix Determinant

Rule: Agar matrix lower/upper triangular ho (diagonal ke upar ya neeche sab zero ho), to determinant = diagonal elements ka product.

1    0    0
2    1    0
1    2    2

Diagonal: 1, 1, 2

Determinant = 1 × 1 × 2 = 2

Shortcut: Triangular matrix → diagonal multiply
